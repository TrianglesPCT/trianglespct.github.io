Items

Dec 23, 2017

Goal: Add a system vaguely in line with minecrafts redstone, but not block based. Instead we will have special items that have a specific behavior, such as switches, detectors(light,sound etc), and power lines(aka redstone dust).

Items triggered by an event

These may be triggered by an input signal event.

  • Bomb: an item that will explode, tearing a hole in the surroundings, and throwing debris
  • Force: an item that will apply a force to anything in front of it. If it is attached to something, it will push it in the opposite direction. Force is based on input power.
  • Light: an item that lights up
  • Sound: an item that makes a specific sound

Signal

Signals are transmitted from item to item. One item may emit a signal that another can process

Lines/Transmitters

Lines are used to attach items to each other, they transmit signals.

These will not be physical power lines like in the real world.

Properties of lines

  • A beam or partical stream will attach the two items.
  • They have a range, if the items become to far apart, the line is broken
  • Some may act on a simple radius

Each item will have attachment slots where a line

Signal gates

These allow for the player to implement basic logic to decide what to do when multiple signals are involved

  • OR:
  • AND
  • XOR
